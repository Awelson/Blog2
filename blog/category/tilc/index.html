    落語 (Rakugo)

    Rakugo (落語) is a form of Japanese verbal comedy where a lone storyteller kneels on a raised platform while telling a story (often comedic in nature) to a public audience1. What I want to talk about today are twofold : One, the etymology behind rakugo, and two, how this relatively ancient form of entertainment has seeped its way into pop culture.
